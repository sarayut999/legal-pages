Frequently asked

  • What files can Crate play? DRM-free audio you own — import via the Files app, or play purchased tracks from your Media Library. DRM-protected tracks are skipped.
  • Where is my music stored? Only on your device. Crate has no servers and no account.
  • What does Crate Pro unlock? The 10-band equalizer, unlimited imports, cloud import, and removes all ads.
  • How do I restore a purchase? Settings → Crate Pro → Restore Purchases.
