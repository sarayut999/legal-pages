FAQ

  • Do I need an account? No — Liftly works offline, no sign-up required.
  • Where is my workout data stored? On your device only, in a local database. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.
  • What happens if I delete the app? Your data is deleted with it — there's no cloud backup yet.
  • What does "Liftly Pro" unlock? Unlimited routines, full history and graphs, next-weight suggestions, and PR cards — a one-time purchase, no subscription.
  • How do I restore my purchase? Settings → Upgrade to Pro → Restore purchase.
  • Can I change kg/lb or bar weight? Yes — Settings → Unit / Bar weight.
